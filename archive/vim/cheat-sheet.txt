insert mode
use 'v' to visual mode
in visual mode
   'd' to cut
   'y' to copy (yank)
   'p' to paste
   '<esc>' to quit

:tabnew [filename] to create new tab
   or tabe
   :tabn to goto next tab
   :tabp to goto previous tab
   gt to go to next tab, gT to goto previous tab
   :tabfirst and :tablast
